Introduction

My name is Prof. Anthony Pannullo. I am an Adjunct at Rowan University's Department of Geography, Planning, and Sustainability. My research centers on the intersection of climate change mitigation, sustainable development, and cooperative frameworks. I develop innovative, data-driven projects aimed at both educational enhancement and practical implementation. I am driven to advance sustainable, actionable insights through projects that are both engaging and scientifically-sound. Overall, my research seeks to make new discoveries in an rapidly-changing world. My goal is to facilitate informed decision-making that promotes sustainability and resilience.
